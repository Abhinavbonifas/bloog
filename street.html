Street Photography

Street photography is all about capturing candid moments in public spaces, showcasing the raw, unfiltered essence of everyday life. Unlike posed photography, it thrives on spontaneity, focusing on the interplay between people and their surroundings. From a lone figure deep in thought to the energy of a bustling city street, these images tell stories through fleeting, unscripted moments. Natural light and real-time shooting play a crucial role in preserving the authenticity of each scene. Imperfections are embraced, as they add to the charm and unpredictability of the genre. To remain unobtrusive, street photographers often blend into the environment, using wide-angle lenses for immersive compositions or telephoto lenses for a more distant perspective.
